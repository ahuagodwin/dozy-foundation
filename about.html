ABOUT DOZY MMOBUOSI FOUNDATION

To promote the progress of Africa and create an environment where Africans can thrive.

The Dozy Mmobuosi Foundation was founded by Dozy Mmobuosi in December 2021 with a noble mission. The Foundation is a non-profit African based foundation committed to providing Unrestricted access to quality healthcare, Educational support, and An enabling environment for Africans to thrive through entrepreneurship and Information Technology development
Our core areas of focus are Healthcare, Education, Entrepreneurship and Information Technology development.

OUR APPROACH TO ACHIEVING OUR GOALS:

  • Go out into the field and listen closely
  • Build strong and supportive relationships with grantees and provide technical assistance
  • Establish a broad consensus for change by creating and encouraging collaboration among the public, private and non-profit sectors in order to leverage the resources that each can bring to common concerns
We believe that everyone deserves a fighting chance. A chance to enjoy life, a chance to live life to the fullest and a chance to be their best, bettering their environment regardless of their location.
We want to play a key role in making this, and many other possibilities, a reality.

Vision

Shape the African society with equity at its core; where everyone has the capacity to live comfortably and be productive in proportion to their highest potential regardless of their location or upbringing.

Mission

  • To encourage overall human development in Africa, with a focus on preference rural regions through strategically placed charitable resources and projects.
  • Promote equity of availability to quality healthcare and healthcare delivery systems in all regions of Africa.
  • Engaging experts within Africa on critical thinking to offer solutions to some of the critical structural problems affecting Africa, especially in rural communities.

Our Values

Our beliefs and characters are the same as that of our founder. These principles shape and guide our work.

Positive Impact

We aim to leave everyone, every place and everything better than when we met it.

Integrity

We act in good faith with uncompromising honesty and integrity in everything we do.

Commitment

We are responsible for our words, our actions, and our results; bringing our A-game Always!
