ABOUT DOZY MMOBUOSI FOUNDATION

Making a difference and creating positive outcomes for less privileged communities across Africa

The Dozy Mmobuosi foundation is a nonprofit African based foundation committed to providing unrestricted access to quality health care facilities, educational support as well as create an enabling environment for Africans to thrive in.
We aim at creating a secure space, free of disease and sickness in order to prolong lives. Supporting rural Africa has always been our dream! The dream to see every part of rural and some urban Africa have access to quality educational with well-equipped schools, access standard health care facilities, provide portable and safe water supply, access modern technology and financial services and many more projects to help achieve a better Africa for us all.
We're building a society where equity and justice can be achievable by having the right mindset and support.

Vision and Mission

Delivering relevant programs for youth that will aid in their successful development

Making a difference that provides youth outreach services that help cultivate the next generation of responsible leaders by providing educational enrichment, mentoring, life transformation skills thereby giving hope again. The Foundation also strives to be a change-agent by fostering excellence in the areas of education, health, and social well-being in urban and diverse communities, ensuring that the needs of the whole child are met
Engaging experts within Africa on critical thinking to profer solutions to some of this critical problems affecting rural communities in Africa.
